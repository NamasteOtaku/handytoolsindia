QR Tracking & Analytics Guide

Setting Up Tracking

  1. Check "Enable Scan Tracking"
  2. Name your campaign for easy identification
  3. Generate your QR code as normal
  4. Click "View Scan Reports" to see your data

Interpreting Your Data

  • Timestamp: Exact scan time (your timezone)
  • Country: Approximate location based on IP
  • Device: Mobile/Desktop/Tablet detection
  • Referrer: Where scan originated (if available)

Google Sheets Tips

  • Create charts: Insert > Chart
  • Filter data: Data > Create filter
  • Set up notifications: Tools > Notification rules
  • Sort by date: Right-click timestamp column

Campaign Optimization

  • Place QR codes in high-traffic areas
  • Test different CTAs ("Scan for discount")
  • Combine with UTM parameters for Google Analytics
  • Use different QR codes for each marketing channel
