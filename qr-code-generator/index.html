QR Code FAQs

What is a QR code?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional barcode that can store various types of information such as URLs, text, contact details, and more. It can be scanned by smartphone cameras.

Are QR codes free to use?

Yes, QR codes are free to generate and use. There are no licensing fees associated with using standard QR codes.

How long do QR codes last?

QR codes themselves don't expire. However, if they point to online content, the content may become unavailable if the website or resource is removed.

Can QR codes be tracked?

Yes, dynamic QR codes with tracking capabilities can monitor scan counts, locations, and devices. Our tool provides basic scan tracking.
