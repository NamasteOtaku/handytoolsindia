QR Code Best Practices & Tips

Why Use QR Codes?

QR codes bridge offline and online experiences. They're perfect for business cards, product packaging, restaurant menus, event promotions, and educational materials. With 75% of smartphone users scanning QR codes, they're an effective engagement tool.

Optimizing QR Codes

  • Maintain a "quiet zone" (blank margin) around the code
  • Test with multiple scanning apps before distribution
  • Add a call-to-action near the code ("Scan to learn more")
  • Ensure sufficient contrast between code and background
  • For print materials, minimum size should be 2x2 cm

Creative Applications

  • Restaurant menus with video recipe links
  • Museum exhibits with extended information
  • Business cards with vCard contact details
  • Product packaging with usage tutorials
  • Event tickets with last-minute updates

Security Considerations

While QR codes themselves are safe, they can direct to malicious sites. Always:

  • Check the URL preview before opening
  • Use QR scanning apps with security features
  • Avoid scanning codes from untrusted sources
  • Never scan codes that look tampered with
