Frequently Asked Questions

What is a text case converter?

A text case converter is a tool that transforms text from one case style to another. It can convert text to uppercase, lowercase, title case, sentence case, and more.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our text case converter is completely free to use with no limitations. There is no registration required.

How does the title case conversion work?

Title case capitalizes the first letter of each word, except for certain small words (like 'a', 'an', 'the') unless they are the first word of the title.

Is the text I convert stored on your servers?

No, all conversions happen directly in your browser. We do not store or transmit any text you enter into this tool.
